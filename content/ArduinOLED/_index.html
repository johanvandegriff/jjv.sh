ArduinOLED is a platform for electronic games and other projects. It includes an OLED screen, a joystick, some buttons, a buzzer, and alligator clip connection points to interface with many other devices. You can see the board design on EasyEDA.

If you have a question or comment, or a project you made with this board, or if you would like to purchase one, send me and email. I would love to add your project to this site. [click to show email address]

Project Ideas

  • Battery load tester for FTC like this one from AndyMark.
  • Games such as whack-a-mole, pong, snake, stacker, connect 4, tic-tac-toe, tetris, asteroids
